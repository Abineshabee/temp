Help & Support

How do I apply for leave?

To apply for leave, click on the "Apply for Leave" button on the dashboard. Fill out the required details including leave type, date range, and reason. Submit your request and wait for approval.

How can I download my reports?

Click on the "Download Report" option in the Quick Actions section. Select the type of report, time period, and format, then click "Generate Report" to download.

How do I update my personal information?

Go to your Profile section by clicking on your profile picture in the top right corner. Select "Edit Profile" and update the necessary information. Click "Save Changes" when done.
